About Us

When you look at society nowadays, you will see inequality and discrimination in all sorts of places. Whether it be at work, school, or even in social media, you are bound to see conflicts that are driven by differences in race, gender, religion, disability, even simple opinions. LILC aims to spread awareness and discourage discrimination across the globe. We do this by showing to the world videos you upload!
